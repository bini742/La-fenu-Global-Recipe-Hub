Pad Thai

Authentic Thai stir-fried noodles with sweet, sour, and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • ¬º cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • ‚Öõ tbsp crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • ¬º cup crushed peanuts
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Equipment

  • Wok or large skillet
  • Wooden spoon
  • Chef's knife

To Serve

  • Lime wedges
  • Extra crushed peanuts
  • Fresh bean sprouts
  • Chili flakes

Tip: Soak noodles in cold water for 30 mins to avoid mushiness.

Instructions

Step 1.

Soak rice noodles in cold water for 30-50 minutes. Drain and set aside.

Step 2.

Heat butter in a wok; cook chicken until browned. Remove and set aside.

Step 3.

Heat oil in the wok. Crack eggs and scramble until firm. Add chicken back.

Step 4.

Add noodles, sugar, fish sauce, vinegar, and red pepper. Stir-fry until tender.

Step 5.

Toss in bean sprouts and cook for 3 minutes.

Step 6.

Garnish with green onions, peanuts, and lemon wedges. Serve hot.
