Quiche Lorraine

⏱️ Prep: 20 mins 🍳 Cook: 45 mins 🍽️ Serves: 6

Classic French tart with bacon, Swiss cheese, and creamy custard.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • ⅓ cup minced onion
  • 1 (9-inch) pie crust
  • 2 cups light cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp sugar
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper

Equipment

  • 9-inch pie dish
  • Skillet
  • Mixing bowl

To Serve

  • Green salad
  • French baguette
  • Fresh fruit

Tip: Let quiche rest for 10 minutes before slicing for cleaner cuts.

Instructions

Step 1.

Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Cook bacon until crispy; chop.

Step 2.

Layer bacon, cheese, and onion in the pie crust.

Step 3.

Whisk cream, eggs, salt, sugar, and cayenne; pour into crust.

Step 4.

Bake at 425°F for 15 mins, then reduce to 300°F for 30 mins.

Step 5.

Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
