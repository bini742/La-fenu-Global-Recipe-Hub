Classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream.

Equipment

  • 9x13 inch baking dish
  • Double boiler
  • Electric mixer
  • Mixing bowls
  • Fine-mesh sieve
  • Rubber spatula

To Serve

  • Extra cocoa powder
  • Chocolate shavings
  • Fresh berries
  • Espresso or dessert wine

Tip: For best results, make tiramisu a day ahead to allow flavors to meld.

Instructions

Step 1.

Whisk yolks and sugar in heatproof bowl over simmering water until pale and thick (about 10 minutes). Remove from heat.

Step 2.

Whisk in milk, then let cool slightly. Fold in mascarpone until smooth.

Step 3.

In separate bowl, whip cream and vanilla to stiff peaks. Gently fold into mascarpone mixture.

Step 4.

Combine coffee and rum in shallow dish. Quickly dip ladyfingers (1-2 seconds per side).

Step 5.

Arrange layer of dipped ladyfingers in dish. Spread half the cream mixture evenly over top.

Step 6.

Repeat with second layer of ladyfingers and remaining cream.

Step 7.

Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours (preferably overnight).

Step 8.

Before serving, dust with cocoa powder through sieve and garnish with chocolate shavings.
