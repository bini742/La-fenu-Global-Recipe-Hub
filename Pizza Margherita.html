Classic Neapolitan pizza with tomato, mozzarella, and fresh basil.

  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarella
  • 20 fresh basil leaves
  • Sea salt to taste

  • Rolling pin
  • Pizza stone or baking sheet

To Serve

Step 1.

Mix flour, salt, water, yeast, and sugar to form dough. Knead for 10 minutes.

Step 2.
