Classic Burger

Juicy, flavorful homemade burgers perfect for summer grilling or stovetop cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Condiments of choice

Equipment

  • Grill or stovetop skillet
  • Spatula
  • Mixing bowl
  • Measuring spoons
  • Meat thermometer (recommended)
  • Cutting board
  • Sharp knife

Tip: For juicier burgers, make a small indentation in the center of each patty before cooking.

Instructions

Step 1.

In a bowl, gently mix ground beef with salt, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder.

Step 2.

Divide mixture into 4 equal portions and form into patties slightly larger than your buns.

Step 3.

Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.

Step 4.

Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness.

Step 5.

If using cheese, add during last minute of cooking to melt.

Step 6.

Toast buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.

Step 7.

Assemble burgers with your preferred toppings and condiments.

Step 8.

Serve immediately while hot.
