Risotto alla Milanese

Creamy Italian saffron risotto with Parmesan and butter.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz (400g) risotto rice (carnaroli or vialone nano)
  • 4 cups (950ml) chicken or vegetable stock
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 2 generous pinches saffron
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1¬Ω oz (40g) freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • ¬Ω cup heavy cream (optional)

Equipment

  • Heavy-bottomed pan
  • Wooden spoon
  • Ladle
  • Fine-mesh strainer

To Serve

  • Extra Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Fresh parsley
  • Crusty bread
  • White wine

Tip: Stir constantly for the creamiest texture.

Instructions

Step 1.

Combine rice and stock in a bowl. Agitate rice to release starch, then strain, reserving liquid.

Step 2.

Heat oil in pan. Toast rice for 5 minutes until nutty. Add onion and cook 1 minute.

Step 3.

Add wine and cook until nearly dry. Pour in most of the stock with saffron and salt.

Step 4.

Cover and cook 10 minutes undisturbed. Stir, then cook 5 more minutes.

Step 5.

Add remaining stock, butter, and cheese. Stir vigorously until creamy.

Step 6.

Fold in whipped cream if using. Serve immediately with extra cheese.
