Japanese-style glazed salmon with miso and fresh garnishes.

Ingredients

  • 275g salmon fillet (skin and bones removed)
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ¬º cup squash and miso mixture
  • 2 tbsp Japanese mayo
  • 60g salmon roe (ikura)

Equipment

  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Small saucepan

To Serve

  • Sushi rice
  • Pea sprouts
  • Roasted squash seeds
  • Shichimi togarashi (optional)

Tip: For best results, marinate the salmon overnight.

Instructions

Step 1.

Combine mirin and sake in a saucepan. Bring to boil, then add soy sauce and honey. Simmer 2-3 minutes. Cool.

Step 2.

Place salmon in a bag with marinade. Remove air and marinate 4+ hours (up to overnight).

Step 3.

Preheat oven to 250¬∞F (120¬∞C). Create a parchment "boat" for salmon on baking sheet.

Step 4.

Bake 35-40 minutes until tender. Spoon juices over, then broil 2 minutes until glazed.

Step 5.

Set the oven to broil and place the salmon back inside. Broil for 2 minutes, or until the surface is glazed and a little browned. Keep an eye on it to avoid burning the salmon. Remove the finished salmon from the oven and set aside to cool.
